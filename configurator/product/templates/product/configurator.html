Flexi Table

The perfect balance of durability, flexibility, and style for your workspace.

Description

The Flexi Table Configurator allows you to design a table perfectly suited to your body size, ensuring better ergonomics, comfort, and productivity in your workspace.

There are two options, either to configure the table based on your body height for two positions, the standard sitting position and the standing position. The software will calculate the most optimal and ergonomic table height for both positions based on your height. It is also possible to manually configure the table by such parameters as length, depth and height.

The main advantages of the table are its relatively cheap and fast production, which can be carried out anywhere in the world. Not only wood can be used as the main material, but also plastic or corrugated cardboard reinforced with mdf boards. In addition, such a table is easy enough to make at home with the necessary materials, tools and instructions.

In future improvements to the website, a function to select the target audience will be added because the target audience of flexi is not only private customers, but also schools, universities and other educational institutions. As it would be more logical for a university to create the appropriate number of students for a range of heights at once. as well as choose colours for the size scale that helps with faster visual search.
